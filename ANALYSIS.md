# Analysis Summary for Apple Inc. (AAPL)

## Company Overview
Apple Inc. is a global technology leader renowned for its iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, services such as the App Store, Apple Music, and iCloud. Founded in 1976, Apple reported FY 2023 revenue of **$383.3B**, net income **$97.0B**, and EPS **$6.13**. The company emphasizes innovation, services growth, and a strong ecosystem.
